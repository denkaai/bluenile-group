About Blue Nile Group

Kenya's Most Trusted Steel Manufacturer

Blue Nile Rolling Mills is a well-known success story among the steel manufacturing sector in Kenya. We believe in installing state-of-the-art technology and keeping on upgrading machinery in due time. This helps us to produce high quality steel and maintain the safety standards required.

Our products are K.E.B.S approved. Over the years the group has developed a large regular client base which includes some of the top business houses and contractors in the country.
